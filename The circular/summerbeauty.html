5 Summer Beauty Trends and How They Influence Packaging

Design directions helping beauty brands create lighter, cleaner and more engaging seasonal packaging experiences.

January 2025 | Packaging Insights

In 2025, beauty packaging continues to evolve beyond aesthetics. Brands are focusing on immersive experiences, tactile finishes, and sustainable materials to create memorable summer-ready unboxing moments.

1. Solid-format beauty products

Solid-format beauty products are gaining popularity for their sustainability and convenience. These compact, mess-free formats reduce packaging waste and appeal to eco-conscious consumers.

2. Layered Packaging and Depth

Structural depth enhances the unboxing journey. Magnetic closures, drawer-style boxes and layered compartments add sophistication and anticipation to the experience.

3. Sustainable Luxury

Sustainability remains central to design. Recyclable materials, minimal waste structures and responsible sourcing are becoming essential elements in premium packaging.

4. Interactive and Experiential Design

QR codes, AR integration and storytelling elements allow customers to engage digitally with physical packaging, strengthening brand connection.
