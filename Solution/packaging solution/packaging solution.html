Our portfolio of innovative, sustainable paper-based packaging solutions prioritizes advanced materials and products that support a circular economy.

As a global leader in sustainable paper-based packaging, we offer an extensive portfolio of solutions—from corrugated boxes and folding cartons to Bag-in-Box packaging—that is designed to promote and protect our customers’ products. With a presence in 40 countries, our holistic approach prioritizes the circular economy, supporting customers in reaching their sustainability goals through renewable, scalable, and efficient packaging. Each product is engineered to safeguard, transport, store and promote goods while minimizing waste and reducing environmental impact across the entire value chain.
